DAS AUTO

Founded in 1937, Volkswagen was established by the German Labour Front with the aim of producing affordable cars for the masses. The iconic Volkswagen Beetle, designed by Ferdinand Porsche, became a symbol of the brand's success. In the post-war era, Volkswagen expanded its lineup with models like the Volkswagen Bus. Throughout the latter half of the 20th century, Volkswagen diversified its offerings with models like the Golf, Passat, and Jetta. In 1998, Volkswagen acquired Audi, marking the beginning of its expansion into the luxury segment. Today, Volkswagen is a global automotive giant, known for its commitment to innovation and sustainability, as seen in its electric vehicle initiatives.
